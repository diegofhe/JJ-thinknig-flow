There are 3 indicators that drives the attacks
1.- Shoulders/hips:
   shoulders in front of hips: Pull attacks
   shoulders in line of hips, or behind: push attacks

2.-Heel/toes: if the bodyweight is
    On the heels: Push attacks
    On the toes: Pull attacks

3.- Heads: if your head is
     Below his: enter below his gravity point, to lift him, like butterfly guards lifts
     Above his: Go for the top positions, like turtle
