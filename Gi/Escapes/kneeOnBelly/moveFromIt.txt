Gi fundamentals vol 7 33:35

1.- With the leg closer to the partner, push him
2.- While doing 1, do a bridge with the other leg, to force partner hands to the ground
3.- With the arm farthest to your partner, grab his knee
4.- Push his knee at the same time you are doing a shrimp or a scissor rotation
5.- With free hand, grab partner heel an pull it, taking him down
